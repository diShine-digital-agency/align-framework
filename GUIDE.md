# Implementation Guide - ALIGN Strategic Framework 🗺️

This guide provides a step-by-step walkthrough of the ALIGN methodology, including templates for internal teams and consultants.

---

## Technical Prerequisites

Before deploying the ALIGN framework, ensure you have access to the following:
- **Baseline Data**: Access to website analytics (GA4/Search Console), CRM wins/losses, and existing sales decks.
- **Stakeholder Access**: Availability of key decision-makers for the "Narrative Alignment Workshop."
- **Customer Interviews**: Permission to interview 3-5 recent customers (wins and losses).

---

## 🧭 Phase-by-Phase Walkthrough

### Phase 1: Assess - Narrative Ecosystem Mapping
The goal is to quantify your current narrative fragmentation.
1. **The Scorecard**: Complete the [Narrative Scorecard](templates/01-assess-narrative-scorecard.md) across all departments.
2. **Customer Validation**: Conduct interviews using the [Interview Guide](templates/narrative-audit-interview-guide.md).
3. **Synthesis**: Identify the gap between internal story and external perception.

**Key Deliverable:** A Narrative Coherence Report.

### Phase 2: Locate - Strategic Positioning
Define where your story has the highest leverage.
1. **Competitive Vector**: Map competitors based on their core narrative (e.g., "Lowest Cost" vs. "Most Innovation").
2. **Value Vector**: Define your "Unique Narrative Vector"—the story no one else is telling.

### Phase 3: Integrate - The Narrative Thread
Embed the story into the organization's DNA.
1. **Messaging Pyramid**: Create a core message hierarchy for the website, sales, and product teams.
2. **The Sales Deck**: Update the core sales narrative based on Phase 2 findings.

### Phase 4: Grow - Strategic Distribution
Accelerate the narrative through content and technology.
1. **MarTech Alignment**: Audit your CRM and marketing automation to ensure they support the new narrative.
2. **Content Strategy**: Develop a content calendar that reinforces the core value vector.

### Phase 5: Nurture - Feedback & Optimization
1. **Recurring Audits**: Re-run the Narrative Scorecard every 6 months.
2. **Market Pulse**: Continuously monitor competitive narrative shifts.

---

## 🏎 Performance Tuning

- **Speed to Value**: If the organization is in crisis, focus on Phase 1 & 2 only to stabilize positioning.
- **Scalability**: For larger organizations, Phase 3 (Integration) should be led by a dedicated "Narrative Owner."
