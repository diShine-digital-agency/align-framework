# ALIGN framework wiki

Welcome to the official wiki for the **ALIGN Strategic Storytelling Framework** — an open-source methodology for diagnosing narrative disconnects and implementing strategic storytelling across B2B digital ecosystems.

This wiki serves as a comprehensive reference companion to the [main repository](https://github.com/diShine-digital-agency/align-framework). It documents the methodology, explains the scientific foundations behind each component, and provides in-depth guidance on strategic storytelling, corporate communications, and AI compliance.
