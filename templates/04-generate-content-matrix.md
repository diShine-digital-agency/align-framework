# Phase 4: GENERATE — Content Narrative Matrix

**Version:** 1.1.0

The fourth phase of the ALIGN Framework focuses on creating the content assets needed to implement your strategic narrative across digital touchpoints.

This matrix translates your Strategic Narrative Blueprint into a concrete content plan, ensuring consistency across channels and journey stages while maximizing audience impact.

---

## How to Use This Matrix

1.  **Map the Journey:** Define the specific stages your buyer goes through (e.g., Awareness, Consideration, Decision, Retention).
2.  **Assign the Narrative Element:** Determine which part of your Strategic Narrative Blueprint (e.g., The Villain, The Guide, The Mechanism) is most relevant at each stage.
3.  **Select the Format:** Choose the content format best suited for that stage and narrative element.
4.  **Define the Core Message:** Write the specific message you want to convey in that asset.

---

## Core Content Assets Checklist

Every B2B company needs a foundational set of assets that consistently deliver the strategic narrative. Ensure you have these covered before scaling your content production:

*   [ ] **The Strategic Homepage:** Clearly articulates the Before/After state and positions you as the Guide.
*   [ ] **The "Why Us" / About Page:** Tells your origin story in a way that proves your empathy and authority.
*   [ ] **The Core Sales Deck:** A 10-15 slide presentation structured around the Hero's Journey or StoryBrand framework.
*   [ ] **The "Villain" Manifesto:** A definitive piece of thought leadership (blog post or whitepaper) that clearly defines the problem you are fighting against.
*   [ ] **3 Transformational Case Studies:** Stories that focus on the customer's journey from pain to success, not just the features they used.
